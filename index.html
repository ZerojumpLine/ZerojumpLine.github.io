Zeju Li is currently a Post-Doctoral Researcher in FMRIB Analysis Group, University of Oxford, working with Prof. Saad Jbabdi. Previously, he obtained his PhD in Computing from BioMedIA Group with Prof. Ben Glocker, Imperial College London. During his PhD, he spent time in MIRACLE Group (Institute of Computing Technology) and Huawei Noah's Ark Lab (London). He got both his MSc and BSc from the Department of Electronic Engineering, Fudan University.

                             

Zeju Li is boardly interested in medical image computing, neuroimaging and machine learning. Currently, Zeju Li is working on building AI models that can 1) operate reliably and stably (e.g. interpretability, uncertainty), 2) advance medical imaging pipelines (e.g. reconstruction, segmentation) 3) and eventually enhance our understanding of brain functions (e.g. fMRI, dMRI).

Actively looking for collabrations on medical image analysis! Please get in touch for brainstorms and discussions!
